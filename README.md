# DanceNow: Your ultimate dance schedule platform in Montreal. 
As a beginner dance amateur, finding and choosing classes can be daunting. DanceNow brings together all dance studios, making it effortless to create your personalized weekly dance schedule. Embrace the rhythm, explore various classes, and never miss a beat with DanceNow! :woman_dancing::man_dancing::stuck_out_tongue_winking_eye:

# Features
 DanceNow offers a wide range of features to elevate your dance experience in Montreal:
<br />
<ul>
  <li> <strong>Customized Schedule Search:</strong> 
    <ul>
      <li>Get the dance schedules easily by entering a specific date, dance type, or preferred studio in the search bar. </li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <br />
  <li><strong>Build Your Personalized Dance Schedule: </strong>
    <ul>
      <li>Create your own weekly dance schedule by selecting classes from various studios. You'll never miss a beat.</li>
    </ul>
  </li>
    <br />
  <li><strong>Convenient Class Booking:  </strong>
    <ul>
      <li>With the "Book" button, reserve your spot directly on the studio's official website. Secure your spot in your favorite classes.</li>
    </ul>
  </li>
   <br />
    <li><strong>Comprehensive Studio Directory: </strong>
    <ul>
      <li>Explore and browse through all participating dance studios in Montreal. Discover new opportunities to expand your dance horizons.</li>
    </ul>
  </li>
</ul>
